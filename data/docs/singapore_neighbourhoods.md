Civic District
Skyscrapers and colonial buildings sit side by side in the Civic District, home to some of the island's finest museums and art galleries.

Dempsey
Surrounded by greenery, Dempsey is a serene, heritage-rich area known for its colonial-style architecture, unique dining, and eclectic lifestyle experiences.

Chinatown
An inspiring blend of hip meets heritage, Chinatown is home to trendy eateries, buzzing watering holes and old-school charm.

Kampong Gelam
Wander from the historic streets of Kampong Gelam to the vibrant Bras Basah and Bugis district, and uncover a world of street art, cultural icons, surprising food finds and hidden gems — set amid temples, museums and contemporary design.

Katong-Joo Chiat
Home to Peranakan culture in Singapore, Katong-Joo Chiat is filled with local flavours, boutique cafes, and charming heritage shophouses.

Little India
Walk the streets of Little India, and discover a harmonious blend of age-old tradition, cutting edge architecture and shops selling traditional garments and intricate jewellery.

Mandai
Ignite your love for nature with the majestic animals, nature-themed attractions and lush greenery at Mandai.

Marina Bay
With its soaring skyscrapers and gorgeous skyline, the Marina Bay we see today is made up of 38 hectares of reclaimed land. 

Sentosa Island
Endless discovery awaits you with Sentosa's golden beaches, tranquil getaways and thrilling theme parks.

Singapore River
From delectable food to buzzing nightlife, the Singapore River's trio of quays are bound to deliver a wave of exciting experiences.

Orchard Road
Orchard Road is the beating heart of Singapore. From luxury shopping to subculture hangouts, there’s a new surprise waiting for you at every corner.
